## 1. Core Resolution Mechanic

**Roll a skill die vs a Difficulty Check (DC).**

- The GM sets a DC and names which of the 9 skills to roll.
- The player rolls the die type they currently have for that skill (d4 through d20).
- **Meet or beat the DC** = success. Below = failure.
- If the player rolls the **maximum value** on their die, the die **Blows Up** (see [Blowing Up](#4-blowing-up)).

## 2. Character Creation

Build your character by choosing:
1. An **Action Hero Name**
2. A **Catchphrase**
3. **3 Abilities** (see [Abilities](#8-abilities-individual))

All 9 skills start at **d4**.

## 3. Skills

There are **9 key skills**. Each is tracked as a die type: d4, d6, d8, d10, d12, or d20.

| Skill | Typical Use |
|---|---|
| **Stunts** | Parkour, acrobatics, daring physical feats |
| **Brawl** | Hand-to-hand combat, grappling, melee fighting |
| **Tech** | Hacking, electronics, technical knowledge |
| **Weapons** | Ranged weapons, armed combat |
| **Sneak** | Stealth, infiltration, hiding |
| **Wits** | Perception, investigation, deduction, memory |
| **Tough** | Endurance, resisting damage, physical resilience |
| **Drive** | Vehicles, piloting, chases |
| **Hot** | Charisma, persuasion, intimidation, social interaction |

Die type progression: **d4 -> d6 -> d8 -> d10 -> d12 -> d20**

## 4. Blowing Up

When a player rolls the **highest value** on their die (4 on d4, 6 on d6, etc.):

1. The die **Blows Up**: keep the current result.
2. The skill **permanently upgrades** to the next die type.
3. **Roll the new die** and add the result to the current check total.
4. If the new roll is ALSO the maximum value, the skill **continues to Blow Up**.
5. Repeat until reaching d20 or rolling below maximum.

> **Note:** When a skill Blows Up, ALL future checks with that skill use the new (higher) die type permanently.

## 5. Prepared Actions

When an Action Hero has **time to prepare** and is **not under stress**:
- Instead of rolling, take **half the die value** for that skill.
- (e.g., d8 Stunts = automatic 4 on a prepared Stunts check)

## 6. Turbo Tokens

**Earning Turbo Tokens:**
- When an Action Hero **fails** a check, they receive **1 Turbo Token**.
- At the **Adrenalized** injury level, the character receives **10 Turbo Tokens**.

**Spending Turbo Tokens (for yourself):**
- Spend **1 Turbo Token** to increase a die roll by **+1** (one-to-one).
- If spending Turbo Tokens brings a roll to its **maximum value**, it triggers a **Blow Up** as if the max had been rolled naturally.
- Spent Turbo Tokens are placed on a player-specific tray.

**Spending Turbo Tokens (for friends):**
- If both characters are **in the same scene**, you can spend Turbo Tokens for a friend at **2x cost** (2 Turbo Tokens = +1 to friend's roll).

**Purchasing Abilities:**
- At the end of each session, remaining Turbo Tokens can buy abilities at **2 Turbo Tokens each** (individual abilities) or **10 per individual / 18 for the whole group** (group suite abilities).

**Special Rules at Severe Injury:**
- At **Severe** injury level, Turbo Tokens cost **double** (2 TT to boost by +1).

## 7. Injury & Tough Checks

When an Action Hero **fails a Tough Check by 5 or more**, they take an **Injury Level**.

### Injury Levels

| Level | Name | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Superficial** | No additional mechanical penalty |
| 2 | **Severe** | Turbo Tokens cost **double** (2 TT per +1). Blowing Up costs double TT as well. |
| 3 | **Adrenalized** | Gain **10 Turbo Tokens**. Next failed Tough Check = **incapacitated or dead** (depending on attacker's intention). |

Cost: **2 Turbo Tokens** each. Characters start with 3 abilities.

| Ability | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Smokin'** | -3 DC on Hot Check when making a first impression |
| **Burglar** | -3 DC on Sneak Check when entering a protected location |
| **Connected** | -3 DC on Hot Check when searching for a helpful ally |
| **Relentless** | Gain 2 Turbo Tokens when you fail a check (instead of 1) |
| **Escape Artist** | -3 DC on Sneak Check when escaping restraints/imprisonment |
| **Flashy** | -5 DC on Hot Check if it's the first roll after you've Blown Up |
| **Transporter** | -3 DC on Drive Check to avoid pursuers |
| **Inspiring** | Each time you succeed, an ally of your choice gets 1 Turbo Token |
| **Loyal** | Your Turbo Tokens give friends +1 at 1:1 rate (instead of 2:1) |
| **Lucky** | Once per episode: spend 2 TT to reroll any check |
| **Trained** | -1 DC for a stat of your choice |
| **Studied** | Replaces Trained. -3 DC for a stat of your choice |
| **Mastery** | Replaces Studied. -5 DC for a stat of your choice |
| **Menacing** | Use Brawl instead of Hot when intimidating an NPC |
| **Nerves of Steel** | Spend 1 TT to treat a snap decision as a Prepared Action |
| **By the Book** | -3 DC on Hot Check when interacting with authority figures |
| **Poker Face** | Use Tough instead of Hot when concealing the truth |
| **Prepared** | GM's Discretion. Spend 2 TT to happen to have one commonplace item |
| **Protector** | -3 DC when defending your friends |
| **Quick Healing** | Recover one injury level at the end of an encounter |
| **Resilient** | Turbo Tokens worth double when boosting against an attack targeting you |

| **Trainer** | Describe a pep talk to spend TT for a friend even when not present |

| **Wealthy** | Spend 1 TT to ease a bad situation with cash |

## 9. Group Suites

Unlocked when **all players** reach a certain die type in at least one skill. Cost: **10 TT per individual** or **18 TT for the entire group**.

### La Familia (Unlocked at d6)

| Ability | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Tough** | Once per episode: Roll Tough on someone else's behalf |
| **Tokens** | Spend tokens for others at 1:1 rate |
| **Skill Die** | Once per episode: Lend a teammate a skill die |

### Criminal Conspiracy (Unlocked at d6)

| Ability | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Item** | When in a new location, produce a single useful item |
| **Tech** | Once per episode: Add your Tech Die to a Sneak Die |
| **Hot** | Roll Hot vs first attack of an encounter to dissuade an opponent |

### Diesel Circus (Unlocked at d8)

| Ability | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Injury Advantage** | Roll twice on first roll after an injury |
| **Double Explosion** | Doubles your amount of tokens |
| **Drive Check** | On a successful Drive Check, make another skill check |

### The Continentals (Unlocked at d8)

| Ability | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Wits** | Turbo Tokens for Wits help increase the die type |
| **Hot Checks** | Two successful Hot Checks auto-succeeds on stealing from an opponent |
| **Melee** | Lower DC of Weapons Checks using melee weapons |

### Alpha Squad (Unlocked at d10)

| Ability | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Group Explosion** | Min 3 people. When everyone uses a different skill in a scene, reduce Blow Up range by 1 |
| **Skill Add** | Once per episode: Two people roll same skill, add totals together |
| **Suit Up** | Once per episode: Group "suits up", each take 2 Turbo Tokens |

### Marauders (Unlocked at d10)

| Ability | Effect |
|---|---|
| **+10** | Beating opponent by 10+ means you defeat an additional opponent |
| **Destroyer** | Gain 1 TT on any turn where you destroy an object |
| **Firestarter** | Start a fire, short circuit electronics, or dissolve a structure as part of any action |

### The Ones (Unlocked at d12)

| Ability | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Max Roll** | Once per episode: Treat a Nat 1 as a max die roll |
| **Reroll** | Once per episode: Reroll a failure with a different skill |
| **Turbo Tokens** | Once per episode: Accept a Nat 1 to gain half the TT of the die value |

### Tactical Command (Unlocked at d12)

| Ability | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Shared Tokens** | Once per episode: Spend Turbo Tokens across scenes |
| **Reroll** | Once per episode: Reroll on a failure |
| **Token Gain** | Characters gain 1 TT at end of scene where they have zero |

### Bustin' Makes Me Feel Good (Unlocked at d20)

| Ability | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Track Restart** | On a Nat 20: Restart that skill track, now rolling two dice and taking the better result |
| **Group Explosion** | On a Nat 20: Everybody else at the table graduates all lowest die types up by one |
| **GM** | On a Nat 20: Become the GM for 60 seconds |
